1. Collection and Processing of Personal Information

In order to better provide you with products and services offered, Callsure AI may collect several different types of data:

1.1 Personal Data

Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment-related information processed securely by third-party providers.
  • Location-based and demographic details related to your use of our services.
  • Behavioral data such as your interactions and preferences within our services.

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information when you visit, use, or navigate our Services. This data may include, but is not limited to:

  • General device and browser characteristics.
  • High-level location data derived from your IP address.
  • Anonymous metrics regarding service performance and engagement.
  • Language preferences
  • Referring URLs, click activity, and usage patterns.

2. Data Processing and Storage

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing necessary for the performance of a contract with you
  • Legal Obligations: Processing necessary for compliance with legal requirements
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing based on our legitimate business interests
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent

2.2 Data Retention Periods

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable laws. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and its usage. In certain cases, data may be retained longer for:

  • Account Information: Duration of account plus 30 days
  • Payment Information: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Usage Logs: 90 days
  • Security Logs: 1 year
  • Backup Data: 90 days

Extended retention may apply for:

  • Legal proceedings
  • Regulatory investigations
  • Security incident investigations
  • Compliance requirements

3. Data Security and Protection

3.1 Technical Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • Internal policies to manage access and monitor usage.
  • Regular evaluations to ensure the security of our systems.

3.2 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Take immediate action to contain the issue and protect your data.
  • Notify affected individuals and authorities as required by applicable laws.

4. Your Rights and Control Over Your Data

4.1 Data Subject Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data within 30 days
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of your data

4.2 Response Times and Procedures

We commit to the following response times:

  • Access Requests: Within 45 days
  • Rectification Requests: Within 30 days
  • Deletion Requests: Within 45 days
  • Portability Requests: Within 45 days
  • Urgent Privacy Concerns: Within 7 days

5. Third-Party Service Providers

5.1 Service Provider Requirements

All third-party service providers must:

  • Sign comprehensive data processing agreements
  • Maintain ISO 27001 certification or equivalent
  • Provide regular security audit reports
  • Implement encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Maintain incident response procedures
  • Provide breach notification within 24 hours

5.2 Current Service Providers

We engage various third-party providers as needed to enhance our services. For details, you may contact us.

6. International Data Transfers

6.1 Transfer Mechanisms

We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Binding Corporate Rules
  • EU-US Data Privacy Framework compliance
  • ISO 27001 certified data centers

6.2 Data Localization

We maintain data centers in multiple regions globally to optimize our services. Data may be stored in the United States or other jurisdictions, depending on operational requirements.
